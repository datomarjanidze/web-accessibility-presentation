<h1>Heading</h1>
<p class="body">...</p>

Heading

Sed ut perspiciatis unde omnis iste natus error sit voluptatem accusantium doloremque laudantium, totam rem aperiam, eaque ipsa quae ab illo

  1. A person with a visual impairment will quickly read the headings with a screen reader to get an idea of a text
  2. SEO will correctly guess where the heading is and that will more likely have a positive influence on how your site is ranked
  3. Also, it will be a bad practice to use a heading element just for the purpose of a bigger font size, where the heading is not intended.
